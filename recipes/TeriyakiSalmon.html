The Teriyaki Salmon

This teriyaki salmon, with its irresistible sweet and savory marinade, has quickly become a staple in my dinner rotation. It's sure to please even the pickiest eaters at my table!

Teriyaki Salmon Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar or mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (optional, for thickening)
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish (optional)
