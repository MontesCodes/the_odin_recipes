The Teriyaki Salmon

This teriyaki salmon, with its irresistible sweet and savory marinade, has quickly become a staple in my dinner rotation. It's sure to please even the pickiest eaters at my table!

Teriyaki Salmon Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar or mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (optional, for thickening)
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1

  2. Step 2

    3. Pour remaining marinade on to a resealable plastic bag. Add salmon steaks, coat with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours.

  3. Step 3

    4. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.

  4. Step 4

    5. Broil salmon steaks under the preheated broiler for 5 minutes, brushing salmon with reserved marinade. Turn and continue to broil until salmon is opaque and flakes easily, about 5 more minutes. Brush with marinade.
