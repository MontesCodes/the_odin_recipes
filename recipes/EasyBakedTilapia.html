The Easy Baked Tilapia

This easy baked tilapia recipe is perfect for a quick and healthy weeknight dinner. With simple ingredients and minimal prep time, it's a delicious way to enjoy tender, flaky fish.

Easy Baked Tilapia Ingredients

  • 4 tilapia fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
