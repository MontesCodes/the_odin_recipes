The Easy Baked Tilapia

This easy baked tilapia recipe is perfect for a quick and healthy weeknight dinner. With simple ingredients and minimal prep time, it's a delicious way to enjoy tender, flaky fish.

Easy Baked Tilapia Ingredients

  • 4 tilapia fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees F). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place tilapia fillets in the bottom of the baking dish and season with seafood seasoning. Melt butter with garlic in a small bowl in the microwave, about 30 seconds on medium; pour over fish. Top each fillet with a slice or two of lemon. Arrange the frozen mixed vegetables around the fish, and season lightly with salt and pepper. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake in the preheated oven until vegetables are tender and fish flakes easily with a fork, 25 to 30 minutes.
